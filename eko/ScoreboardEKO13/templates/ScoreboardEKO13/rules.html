Goal

Find vulnerabilities in any voting related software or hardware from Argentina.

Assets

Regarding Vot.Ar system, the one used in CABA 2015, Salta since ~2009 and many more places, you can get:

  • the source code of the voting software: https://github.com/prometheus-ar/vot.ar
  • the ISO of the voting and counting software (same as the one above): https://anonfile.com/S747o4ccbe/votar_salta_paso_2017.iso
  • the ISO of the votes transmission system: https://anonfile.com/e4H5m9c0b7/votar_transmision_corrientes.iso

Analyze those source codes, find vulnerabilities and win. The one with the highest score by the end of the Ekoparty gets a nice prize!

Rules

  1. There's no registration needed: whatever you find, present it at the stand or email it to hacktheprinter@protonmail.com. They will be analyzed by a board who decides if they are valid.
  2. You get points for vulnerabilities and exploits: 1 point for each.
  3. The one with the highest score gets the CTF Flag. There's gonna be one Flag per day unless nobody finds anything.
  4. DON'T CHEAT! You cannot repeat vulns found by other teams or individuals.
  5. DO NOT ATTACK THE SCORING SERVER IN ANY WAY! You'll be banned and kicked out.
